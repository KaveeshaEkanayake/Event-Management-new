About Us

Dreamscape Events is a leading event management company dedicated to creating extraordinary and memorable experiences for clients. We specialize in corporate events, weddings, trade fairs, and private celebrations.

Our Vision

"To be the leading trade fair organizer and the top MICE solutions provider in the region."

Mission

To achieve customer satisfaction by delivering a professional and an accurate service while increasing the productivity and ensuring the development of the employees.

About Our Company

Dreamscape was established with the ambition to be a leading exhibition organizer in Sri Lanka. A member of Connaissance De Ceylan Ltd, Dreamscape Events is manned by most experienced professionals who had been in the industry for more than decades and served the Industry in National and Regional Level. With over a decade of experience, our team is made up of seasoned professionals who have planned and executed events of all scales. We bring creativity and a meticulous approach to every event, ensuring it runs flawlessly.

Company Values

At Dreamscape Events, we value integrity, innovation, customer satisfaction, and sustainability. Our dedicated team is passionate about creating events that leave a lasting impression.
